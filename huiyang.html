Game Tips

You're given a recipe, a list of orders, and a time limit to complete them in. You pull the appropriate ingredients from a container, chop them up, cook them, and then serve the finished dish on a plate to hungry diners.

Being 'All You Can Eat' we have more levels than a buffet table has meals! Over 200+ Campaign levels can be found throughout the Extra Trimmings Menu and the remasters of the two Overcooked! Campaigns.

At the main menu, look to the bottom-right corner where it says ``Invite Friends /_'', so press triangle. Below that is ``Player 2 Press X to join locally'', do so and the first open slot will highlight orange. Press X again and select split controller.
